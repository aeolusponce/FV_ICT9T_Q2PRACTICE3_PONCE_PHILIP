Haikyuu!!


Synopsis:


Haikyuu!! is a sports anime and manga series that focuses on a highschoool boys' volleyball team. The main character, Hinata Shoyo, though he may not have the height for volleyball, his determination makes it possible with dreams of becoming an ace like the "Little Giant" who he idolized. Later in the series he joins Karasuno High, where he unexpectedly must partner with his rival, Tobio Kageyama, a prodigy setter. Together they learn the value of teamwork, communication, and reality. Haikyuu!! is known for its realistic portrayal of volleyball and how hardwork can match people with talent. With its excellent character development, it inspires viewers with passion.
